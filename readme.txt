Yo Gallery - modern, stylish, simple and very flexible wordpress gallery plugin

== Description ==   

Yo Gallery - it's a simple and very effective wordpress gallery plugin with simple set of the options and simple image management tools. 

Yo Gallery have wide range of the functionality and Add-ons.  Yo gallery could be simply installed in few steps and in few minutes you can publish image gallery widget. In close future we have plans to publish wide range of add-ons for our gallery. In additional plugins you can find wide range of the thumbnail layouts, styles for all gallery elements, menu styles, breadcrumbs and pagination buttons, albums, lightboxes,  hover effects , integration with social media resources.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= 1. How to enable Yo gallery for main wordpress gallery? =

When you open wordpress admin section move to the Settings section / Yo Gallery an swith enable option on this section.

= 2. How to upload Yo gallery images? =

When you open wordpress widgets section on the left side with the list of the widgets yo ucan find Yo gallery widget. Just drag and drop widget. Whe nyou click on widget you'll see additional options. Click on manage images button  and use this section for upload and manage images of the gallery.

= 3. How to change columns in Yo gallery grid? =

When you open wordpress widgets section on the left side with the list of the widgets yo ucan find Yo gallery widget. Just drag and drop widget. Whe nyou click on widget you'll see additional options. When you open Yo gallery widget settings you can switch between columns amount of the Yo gallery grid.
